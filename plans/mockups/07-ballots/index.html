Journey 7: Standards Ballot

Compare Three Design Approaches for Standards Development Ballot Management

The Challenge

Standards Working Group Chairs need to manage complex ballot processes that involve document versioning, inline commenting with precise line references, systematic comment resolution, change tracking across iterations, and approval voting. The interface must balance the need to see the document in context, efficiently process high volumes of comments, and track what changed between versions. How should we design this multi-faceted workflow?

Option A

Document-Centric View

Keep the document front and center with integrated commenting and resolution tools. Comments appear inline with the text, preserving context while enabling efficient resolution.

  • Full document display with line numbering
  • Inline comment markers and threading
  • Sidebar for comment details and resolution
  • In-place editing and change highlighting
  • Integrated voting panel

Best For

  • Seeing comments in context
  • Detailed line-by-line review
  • Sequential comment resolution
  • Real-time collaborative editing

  • Analytics and progress tracking

Best For

  • High-volume comment processing
  • Systematic resolution workflows
  • Committee-based review assignments
  • Progress tracking and reporting
