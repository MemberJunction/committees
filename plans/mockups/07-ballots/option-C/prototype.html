Draft 3 (Revised) v2.0-draft3
1 1. Introduction
2 This standard establishes security requirements for Internet of Things (IoT) devices deployed in enterprise environments.
3 It applies to all connected devices that collect, process, or transmit data classified as Confidential or higher. 1
4
5 2. Scope
6 This standard covers the following device categories:
7 • Industrial sensors and actuators
8 • Smart building systems
9 • Healthcare monitoring devices
10 • Edge computing devices 2
11
12 3. Security Requirements
13 3.1 Authentication
14 All IoT devices SHALL implement multi-factor authentication for administrative access.
15 Default passwords MUST be changed during initial device setup.
16 Devices SHALL support certificate-based authentication for machine-to-machine communication.
17 3.2 Encryption
18 Data in transit MUST be encrypted using TLS 1.3 or higher.
19 Data at rest MUST be encrypted using AES-256 or equivalent. 3
