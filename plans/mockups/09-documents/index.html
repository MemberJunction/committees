Journey 9: Document Collaboration

Compare Three Design Approaches

User Journey Overview

Scenario: A committee member needs to access shared meeting folders, review and comment on the agenda document, upload supporting materials for discussion, track changes made to documents over time, and receive notifications when other members update or comment on documents.

This journey focuses on seamless document collaboration within the committee workflow, ensuring all members can efficiently prepare for meetings, contribute materials, and stay synchronized with document updates.
