Evaluation Criteria

Usability

How quickly can users find items, update status, and add documentation? How intuitive is the interface?

Efficiency

How many clicks for common tasks? How well does it support bulk operations and filtering?

Information Architecture

How well organized is the information? How clear are relationships and dependencies?

Flexibility

How well does it adapt to different workflows and user types? How customizable is the view?
